Check and Validate your NRIC numbers in bulk

What does checkNRIC do?

checkNRIC uses the Singapore NRIC Check Digit / Checksum algorithm, available online and popularly used as a programming challenge, to check the validity of any 9-digit Singapore NRIC number. It works for both Singapore NRIC and FIN numbers. We are not affiliated to the Singapore Government in any way.

What does a valid NRIC number mean here?

It means that the last checksum alphabet of the NRIC number matches the 7-digit NRIC number and the prefix alphabet. It does not mean that the NRIC number actually belongs to a real person, and we do not guarantee that it does.

Why was checkNRIC created?

We realised a need for a quick and user-friendly tool to bulk-validate NRIC numbers, especially for administrative staff who deal with large numbers of personal particulars and who do not have direct access to official systems to check if a provided NRIC number is indeed a valid one. While we do not guarantee the validity of the NRIC number against a real person, we hope this tool would serve as a quick validation tool to help you identify possible errors in your data and save you some precious time from doing manual verification.

Why do I have to go through the CAPTCHA validation once in a while?

This is to prevent abuse of our system. The reCAPTCHA validation should be easy enough for humans to complete.

Will using this tool compromise my personal data?

You are not required to provide any personal information to use this tool, and we do not store identifying information in our server.
